## The Problem: Analysis Paralysis
In the fast-paced world of technology, developers and decision-makers face an overwhelming number of choices daily. React or Vue? AWS or Azure? SQL or NoSQL? 
Traditional research involves opening twenty tabs, reading conflicting articles, and trying to synthesize a conclusion. This leads to **Analysis Paralysis**—weeks wasted on decisions that should take hours.

## The Solution: The Referee
"The Referee" is an AI-powered decision support tool that doesn't just list specs—it calls the winner. By inputting two options and your specific context (e.g., "Startup with limited budget", "Enterprise needing high security"), The Referee analyzes the trade-offs and provides:
1.  **A Clear Verdict**: Who wins for your specific case.
2.  **Contextual Summary**: Why they won.
3.  **Head-to-Head Breakdown**: A category-by-category comparison.

## How It Works
The application is built with a modern React frontend and leverages AI for the logic engine.
-   **Frontend**: Vite + React for a snappy, premium experience.
-   **Styling**: Custom CSS variables for a "Dark Mode" aesthetic that feels professional and high-tech.
-   **Logic**: An intelligent service that weighs constraints against features.

### Key Features
-   **Context Awareness**: A "React vs Vue" comparison yields different results for a "Solo Dev" vs a "Meta Enterprise".
-   **Visual Breakdown**: Easy-to-scan comparison cards.
